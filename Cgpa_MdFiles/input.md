### BSc Degree Level (BSc in Programming and Data Science)
This level requires completion of the Foundation and both Diplomas (86 credits prerequisite). It includes 4 core courses (16 credits), 1 mandatory course (4 credits), and electives (up to 8 credits, transferable from NPTEL), for a total of 28 credits at this level.

| Subject Code | Subject Name | Credits | Type |
|--------------|--------------|---------|------|
| BSCS3001 | Software Engineering | 4 | Core |
| BSCS3002 | Software Testing | 4 | Core |
| BSCS3003 | AI: Search Methods for Problem Solving | 4 | Core |
| BSCS3004 | Deep Learning | 4 | Core |
| BSGN3001 | Strategies for Professional Growth | 4 | Mandatory |
| Varies | Electives (from NPTEL) | Up to 8 | Elective |

**Total Credits for BSc Degree Level**: 28  
**Cumulative Credits for BSc Degree**: 114 (Foundation 32 + Diplomas 54 + BSc Level 28)

### BS Degree Level (BS in Data Science and Applications)
This level requires completion of the BSc (114 credits prerequisite). It includes the same 4 core courses as BSc (16 credits, but counted here if not already), 1 mandatory course (4 credits), electives (up to 8 from NPTEL, up to 12 from apprenticeship), and additional electives (e.g., Algorithmic Thinking in Bioinformatics, Big Data and Biological Networks, etc.), for a total of 28 credits at this level.

The core and mandatory are shared/overlapping with BSc, but additional electives make up the difference. Specific additional electives include:
- Algorithmic Thinking in Bioinformatics (4 credits)
- Big Data and Biological Networks (4 credits)
- And others (not fully listed; total electives flexible to reach 28 credits).

**Total Credits for BS Degree Level**: 28  
**Cumulative Credits for BS Degree**: 142 (BSc 114 + BS Level 28)
